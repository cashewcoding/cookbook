GARLIC NAAN

Delicious Indian Naan bread for you, your friends and your whole family

KEY FACTS

  • Total Time

    2 Hours 5 minutes:
    *15 mins prep / 20 mins cook
    / 1 hr 30 mins rest

  • Servings

    8 naan
YOU WILL NEED THIS MUCH
All Purpose Flour (maida) 3.25 cups + 3-4 tbsp
Salt 1 tsp
Water (lukewarm) 1 cup
Sugar 1 tbsp
Active Dry Yeast 2 tsp
Milk (lukewarm) 1/3 cup
Plain Yogurt 2 tbsp
Oil 3 tbsp
Garlic Gloves 2 (large)
Butter 3 tbsp
Garlic 2 tsp (minced)

TO DO THIS

  1. In a large mixing bowl, combine 3.25 cups All Purpose Flour (maida), 1 tsp Salt, 1 tbsp Sugar, and 2 tsp Active Dry Yeast. Mix well.
  2. In a separate bowl, combine 1/3 cup Lukewarm Milk, 2 tbsp Plain Yogurt, and 3 tbsp Oil. Mix well.
  3. Add the wet ingredients to the dry ingredients, and knead for about 8-10 minutes, or until the dough becomes smooth and elastic. If the dough is too sticky, add 3-4 tbsp of All Purpose Flour.
  4. Place the dough in a greased bowl, cover it with a kitchen towel or plastic wrap, and let it rise for about 1 hour, or until doubled in size.
  5. Preheat your oven to 500°F (250°C).
  6. Crush 2 large Garlic Cloves and add it to 3 tbsp Butter. Microwave the butter mixture for 20-30 seconds or heat it in a pan until the butter is melted.
  7. After the dough has risen, punch it down and divide it into 8-10 equal parts. Roll each part into a ball and then roll it out into a circular shape, about 1/4 inch thick.
  8. Brush the naan with the garlic butter mixture and then place it on a baking sheet or pizza stone.
  9. Bake the naan in the preheated oven for about 5-7 minutes, or until the top is puffed and lightly browned.
  10. Remove the naan from the oven and brush it with remaining garlic butter mixture, and sprinkle 2 tsp minced Garlic on top of it.
  11. Serve the naan immediately while it's hot, you can enjoy it with a variety of dips or curries.
