Fried Eggs

Simply the best when you are hungry and have nothing else but eggs at home

KEY FACTS

  • Total Time

    5 minutes:
    *0 mins prep / 5 mins cook
    / 0 mins rest

  • Servings

    1 person
YOU WILL NEED THIS MUCH
Eggs 3
Salt 1 pinch
Pepper 1 pinch

TO DO THIS

  1. Heat pan
  2. Crack the eggs
  3. Let the eggs slowly slide into the pan from their shells
  4. Fry for about 5 minutes until they look like you want them to look
  5. Optional: turn eggs over for over-easy eggs
  6. Enjoy!
