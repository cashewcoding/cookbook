GARALIC NAAN
  • Total Time: 2 Hours 5 minutes

    • *15 mins prep / 20 mins cook / 1 hr 30 mins rest

  • Serves: 8 naan
INGREDIENTS QUANTITY
All Purpose Flour (maida) 3.25 cups + 3-4 tbsp
Salt 1 tsp
Water (lukewarm) 1 cup
Sugar 1 tbsp
Active Dry Yeast 2 tsp
Milk (lukewarm) 1/3 cup
Plain Yogurt 2 tbsp
Oil 3 tbsp
Garlic Gloves 2 (large)
Butter 3 tbsp
Garlic 2 tsp (minced)
